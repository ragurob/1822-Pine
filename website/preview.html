The Property at a Glance

Historic Significance

  • Built 1854, documented provenance
  • 1899 renovation by Duhring, Okie & Ziegler
  • Protected historic district property
  • Original marble mantels & millwork

Accommodations

  • 5 distinct suites across 5 levels
  • Sleeps 16-18 guests comfortably
  • Full chef's kitchen & butler's pantry
  • Double parlors with 14-foot ceilings

Location

  • 2 blocks from Rittenhouse Square
  • Walk to 30+ restaurants
  • Near Walnut Street shopping
  • Easy access to museums & theaters

Amenities

  • High-speed WiFi throughout
  • Central air & heating
  • Washer/dryer on premises
  • Private garden courtyard

House Rules & Policies

As a Limited Lodging operator in Philadelphia, we maintain specific requirements:

  • Occupancy: Maximum 3 unrelated adults OR one family unit
  • Visitors: Permitted 8 AM - midnight only
  • Events: No parties or commercial events
  • Check-in: 4 PM | Check-out: 11 AM
  • Smoking: Not permitted anywhere on property
  • Pets: Not permitted
