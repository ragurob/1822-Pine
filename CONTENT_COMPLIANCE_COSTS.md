## I. PHILADELPHIA SHORT-TERM RENTAL COMPLIANCE

### A. Required Licenses & Permits

Based on current Philadelphia regulations, 1822 Pine Street requires:

| License/Permit | Type | Cost | Renewal |
|----------------|------|------|---------|
| **Commercial Activity License (CAL)** | Business registration | Free | Annual |
| **BIRT Account** | Business Income & Receipts Tax | Free | Ongoing |
| **Rental License (Hotel Use)** | Non-primary residence | $63/unit | Annual |
| **Use Registration Permit** | Visitor Accommodations | Varies | Annual |
| **Lead Safety Certification** | Pre-1978 building | ~$300-500 | As required |

**Classification:** Since this is NOT a primary residence and operates >180 days/year, it falls under **"Visitor Accommodation"** (hotel-style), not "Limited Lodging."

**Virtual Inspections:** Starting July 1, 2025, Philadelphia conducts virtual inspections during license application/renewal. Property must demonstrate:
- Smoke alarms in each bedroom + hallways + every floor
- CO alarms within 15 feet of bedroom entrances
- Fire extinguishers accessible
- Lead safety certification (pre-1978 building)

### B. Tax Obligations

| Tax | Rate | Collected By | Due |
|-----|------|--------------|-----|
| **Philadelphia Hotel Tax** | 8.5% | Host or Airbnb | Monthly (15th) |
| **PA State Hotel Occupancy** | 6% | Airbnb (if booked through) | Quarterly |
| **PA Additional Local (Philly)** | 1% | State-collected | Quarterly |
| **Business Income & Receipts Tax (BIRT)** | 5.99% net income | Host | Annual |
| **Net Profits Tax (NPT)** | 3.75% (resident) | Host | Annual |

**Total Guest-Facing Tax:** ~15.5% (collected at booking)

**What's Taxable:**
- Nightly rate
- Cleaning fees
- Pet fees
- Extra person fees
- Early check-in/late checkout fees
- No-show fees

**Not Taxable:**
- Cancellation fees (if no stay occurred)
- Security deposit (if refunded)

### C. Compliance Display Requirements

**Must Display on All Listings:**
- License number (Rental License for Hotel Use)
- Business registration number
- Maximum occupancy (16-18 guests)

**Website Compliance Section (`/policies/compliance`):**
- License numbers
- Tax registration confirmation
- Safety certifications
- Historic district designation
- Insurance summary (public-safe version)

## II. WEBSITE CONTENT STRATEGY

### A. Pre-Booking Content (Sell the House)

**Goal:** Convert browsers to bookers while setting accurate expectations

#### Homepage Messaging Hierarchy
1. **Hero:** Emotional hook + key differentiators
   - "A Philadelphia Landmark Since 1854"
   - "8 Bedrooms • Rittenhouse Square • 198 Years of History"

2. **Trust Signals:**
   - "Licensed & Insured Vacation Rental"
   - "4.9★ Average Guest Rating"
   - "Connected to the Drexel Banking Dynasty"
   - "Featured in [Publications]"

3. **Value Proposition:**
   - Perfect for family reunions, corporate retreats, wedding parties
   - Steps from Rittenhouse Square's restaurants and shops
   - The most documented historic home in Philadelphia

#### Property Pages Content

**What to Include:**
| Page | Content | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| `/stay` | Room-by-room tour, amenities, bed configurations | Set expectations |
| `/stay/floor-plans` | Interactive floor plans, PDF downloads | Group planning |
| `/history` | Full 19-chapter narrative, 65 documents | Build emotional connection |
| `/gallery` | 149+ images (property + historic) | Visual sell |
| `/neighborhood` | Walking distances, restaurant picks, attractions | Location value |
| `/events` | Capacity, layout options, inquiry form | Event/filming leads |

**What NOT to Include (Pre-Booking):**
- Exact street address (show neighborhood only)
- Lock codes or access details
- Specific security system info
- Staff schedules
- Utility locations

#### Pricing Page Content (`/rates`)

**Transparent Pricing:**
```
Base Rate: $1,600 - $2,500/night
- Weekday (Sun-Thu): $1,600
- Weekend (Fri-Sat): $2,200
- Peak/Holiday: $2,500+
- Event Premium: +$500

Additional Fees:
- Cleaning Fee: $450 (flat)
- Pet Fee: $150/pet (max 2)
- Extra Guest Fee: $50/person (over 12)

Taxes: 15.5% (Philadelphia + PA)

Minimum Stay: 2 nights (3+ on holidays)
```

**Deposit & Cancellation:**
- Security Deposit: $1,000 (via Truvi, refundable)
- Cancellation: Full refund 30+ days, 50% 14-30 days, no refund <14 days
