# Operations Manual Brief - 1822 Pine Street

This is the definitive operations brief for a premium short-term rental managed in **Guesty**, with an on-site cleaning team and a high standard for guest experience, safety, and asset preservation. It is structured to become the full operations manual and house manager guide.

---

## 1. Objectives and Success Metrics

**Primary objectives**
- Deliver a zero-surprise guest experience.
- Protect the property and reduce insurance risk.
- Run operations with predictable, documented workflows.
- Maximize revenue through smart pricing and upsells.

**KPIs**
- Response time: under 10 minutes during guest hours
- Turnover time: same-day readiness for standard turnovers
- Cleanliness score: 4.9+ average across platforms
- Issue resolution: 95% resolved within 4 hours
- Upsell conversion: 10%+ of bookings
- Incident rate: zero repeat incidents of the same type

---

## 2. Core Operating System (Guesty-Centered)

**System of record**: Guesty (reservations, messaging, tasks, rates, owner reporting)

**Single source of truth**
- All reservations, messages, and tasks live in Guesty.
- All cleaning checklists and inspection photos map to Guesty reservations.
- All asset data, vendor contacts, and SOPs live in the House Manager Guide.

## 5. Cleaning and Turnover Checklists

**Checklist app requirement**: Mobile checklists with photos and timestamps.

**Best picks**
- **Breezeway** (primary) for checklists, inspections, and issue tracking
- **Turno** (optional) for scheduling and cleaner payments if you want a marketplace

**Core turnover checklist (summary)**
- Entry: doors, locks, welcome areas, stairwell
- Bedrooms: linens, pillows, beds, dust, floors, mirrors
- Bathrooms: sanitize, replace supplies, check plumbing
- Kitchen: appliances, dishes, counters, trash
- Common areas: upholstery, surfaces, remotes, lighting
- Outdoor: trash bins, doorbell, entry lighting
- Systems: Wi-Fi test, HVAC setpoints, Minut and Ring status
- Photo log: 12 required photos (key rooms + any issues)
